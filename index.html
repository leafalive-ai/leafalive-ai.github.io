Getting Started

How it works

From unboxing to your first conversation in minutes.

1

Place the Sensor

Nestle the small wireless sensor into the soil of your houseplant. It immediately starts reading moisture, light, temperature, and humidity.

2

Snap a Photo

Take a photo of your plant in the app. AI identifies the species, fetches ideal care ranges, and generates a charming Ghibli-style avatar.

Start Talking

Your plant now has a voice. It tells you when it's thirsty, asks about your day, and becomes a gentle companion you look forward to checking on.
